BS Cyber Security

Defend critical digital infrastructures from advanced persistence threats and elite hackers.

Program Overview

Our BS in Cyber Security program bridges the severe skills gap in global network defense and enterprise security. You will dive deeply into digital forensics, cryptography, malware analysis, and network defense architectures. Conduct live-fire attacks and defenses in our isolated virtual playground, learning offensive strategies to better implement defensive measures.

What You Will Learn

Network Security

Design end-to-end trusted networks resilient to MITM and DDoS attacks.

Modern Cryptography

Understand post-quantum cryptographic primitives and encryption algorithms.

Ethical Hacking

Learn penetration testing frameworks and vulnerability exploitation.

Digital Forensics

Trace breaches, reverse-engineer malware, and provide incident response.
