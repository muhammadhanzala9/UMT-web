Program Overview

The BS in Informatics explores how people and systems intersect. The program teaches the design, application, and utilization of information technology to solve complex problems in business, healthcare, and society. You will focus heavily on User Experience (UX), Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and Information Architecture.
