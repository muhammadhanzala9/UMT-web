BS Artificial Intelligence

Pioneering cognitive automation, deep learning, and advanced robotics to solve the impossible.

Program Overview

The BS in Artificial Intelligence empowers students to construct intelligent agents that simulate natural human cognition. Covering everything from machine learning models and computer vision to advanced reinforcement learning, our program prepares you to build AI infrastructure. Students engage in practical lab research utilizing modern GPU clusters and work directly on autonomous models.

What You Will Learn

Machine Learning Basics

Implement regressions, SVMs, and decision trees using Python frameworks.

Computer Vision

Analyze image pixels, object detection, and spatial recognition using CNNs.

Natural Language Processing

Design LLMs, transformers, and sentiment analysis pipelines.

Robotics & Actuation

Bridge the physical and digital with real-world agent movement planning.

Career Prospects

AI Research Scientist
Machine Learning Engineer
Data Engineer
Computer Vision Architect
Robotics Programmer
