BS Computer Science

Building the robust architecture of the future through scalable software and complex systems engineering.

Program Overview

The BS in Computer Science program is meticulously designed to equip motivated students with a deep, mathematical foundation in computational theory, software engineering, and modern technology paradigms. Rather than just learning to code, our graduates learn to design, optimize, and analyze complex software systems to solve real-world global problems. The curriculum strictly adheres to international standards and evolves rapidly with industry demands.

What You Will Learn

Advanced Algorithms

Master data structures, algorithm optimization, and computational complexity.

Systems Architecture

Deep dive into distributed systems, operating systems, and computer networks.

Full-Stack Engineering

Build robust enterprise web applications using modern scalable frameworks.

Cloud Infrastructure

Deploy and manage high-availability clusters and enterprise databases.
