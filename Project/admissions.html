Application Timeline

Follow these critical steps to ensure your application is processed promptly for the upcoming semester.

1. Create a Secure Profile

Register on the UMT admissions portal, verify your email address, and setup two-factor authentication for your applicant account.

2. Complete Academic History

Enter your high school or undergraduate GPA, exact courses taken, and write an intentional statement of purpose (SOP).

3. Upload Official Documents

Upload high-resolution scans of your transcripts, passport/national ID, and recent photographs. File verification takes 48 hours.

4. Application processing fee

Pay the non-refundable fee ($50 for domestic applicants, $100 for international) via secure cardiovascular portal or bank transfer.

5. Technical Assessment / Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an online logical reasoning test followed by a behavioral panel interview.
